Build with AI: Gemini Hackathon with DeepMind 🚀

Participant Info Pack
Hello Hackers! We are officially counting down to the main event. To ensure you have a smooth experience, we’ve summarized the theme, schedule, judging criteria, and submission requirements below.

🌟 1. Overall Theme
Build innovative solutions that have not existed yet. Leverage Gemini’s intelligence to solve uniquely Malaysian challenges and create a future that is smarter, more inclusive, and resilient.

💡 2. Problem Statements
Innovation doesn't always fit into a box, but we have identified four key tracks relevant to the Malaysian landscape. Choose one that inspires you:
Digital Economy & Smarter SMBs: Building tools for Malaysia’s 1.2 million+ SMEs to automate customer service, optimize local supply chains, or generate localized marketing content in Bahasa Melayu, English, and Mandarin.
Examples: AI Tools for local SMEs, Customer Automation, Localized Marketing.
Education & Skill Upgrading: Leveraging Gemini’s multimodal capabilities to create personalized learning tools for Malaysian students or professional upskilling platforms that bridge the local tech talent gap.
Examples: AI Tutors, Upskilling Platforms, Learning Assistants.
Sustainable Urban Living: Solutions for smart cities—think traffic management in KL, flash flood early warning systems using satellite data, or AI-driven energy conservation for high-rise living.
Examples: Traffic Management, Disaster Warnings, Smart Energy Use.
Cultural Heritage & Inclusivity: Preserving and promoting Malaysian culture through AI—language translation for local dialects, accessibility tools for the elderly, or digitizing historical archives.
Examples: Dialect Translation, Accessibility tools, Cultural Archive Digitalization.
Others: If you see a gap in the Malaysian landscape—be it in healthcare, fintech, or agritech—that isn't covered above, this is your space to innovate.

📤 5. Submission Checklist
Submissions will be managed through Devpost. The submission link will be sent to all participants tomorrow.
Required Deliverables:
Project Details: Your project name and a high-level summary.
Technical Explanation: A breakdown of your architecture and tech stack.
Prompt Design Explanation: Detail your prompt engineering process and how you steered the AI.
GitHub Repository Link: Ensure your repository is public or shared with judges.
1-Minute Demo Video: A link to a recording demonstrating your project’s core value.
Deployed Application Link: A live URL for judges to test.
Note: We highly encourage deployment on Google Cloud Run, but any similar service is acceptable.
